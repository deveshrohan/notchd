Features

🖥

Notch-native overlay

The panel extends behind the MacBook notch, merging seamlessly with the Dynamic Island aesthetic.

🖱

Hover to open

Move your cursor near the notch and the panel glides open. Move away and it dismisses — no clicks needed.

🔥

Animated streak flame

Lottie-powered fire animation that scales in intensity with your streak — from a flicker at 1 day to a roaring flame at 30+.

✦

Grid entrance animation

Contribution cells pop in randomly on open with an ongoing blink effect — satisfying every single time.

🔒

Keychain-secured token

Your GitHub PAT is stored in macOS Keychain — never in plaintext, never leaves your machine.

⚡

Lightweight

2.4 MB DMG. No background agents. No telemetry. Sandboxed with minimal permissions.

Get started in 3 steps

1

Download & install

Open the DMG and drag gitTracker.app to your Applications folder.

2

Add your credentials

Click the menu bar icon → gear icon → enter your GitHub username and a Personal Access Token with read:user scope.

3

Hover the notch

Move your cursor toward the Dynamic Island — your contribution graph appears instantly.
